Hardened by Privacy.

AURA is designed around local control. The local LLM understands home commands on your network, then sends actions to smart appliances without relying on a cloud assistant for routine automation.

Local LLM Command Engine

AURA interprets spoken or typed commands locally and converts them into appliance actions. It can route requests like lights, fans, plugs, and scenes to the correct smart-home device on your network.

Home Network Control

The system focuses on devices inside the house: smart bulbs, fans, plugs, thermostats, and other connected appliances. Commands are resolved locally before actions are sent to the devices.

The goal is simple: routine home commands should not need to leave the home. AURA keeps language understanding and device routing local so appliance control remains responsive even when internet services are unavailable.
